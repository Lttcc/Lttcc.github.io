Biography

Currently I am a graduate student of School of Artificial Intelligence in Nanjing University and a member of LAMDA Group, led by professor Zhi-Hua Zhou.

I received my B.Sc. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University in June 2021. In September 2021, I was admitted to pursue a M.Sc. degree in Nanjing University, under the supervision of Associate Professor Zongzhang Zhang.
